Bill Kasel Biography

Early professional work for Bill included playing with Sarah Caldwell and the Opera Company of Boston, the Empire Brass Quintet and many orchestras in Boston. William has given recitals in New England and Pennsylvania. Bill is currently a member of the Concord Orchestra and performs professionally in the New England area.

He began playing trumpet in the 4th grade, and with that discovered his passion. As a high school student, William studied under Dr. Kenneth Laudermilch of West Chester University. William was a 1979 winner of the Lancaster Historical Society solo performance competition and a scholarship recipient which was followed by an invitation to attend the Boston University Tanglewood Institute in Lenox, Massachusetts where he studied with Armando Ghitalla, principal trumpet of the Boston Symphony Orchestra.
